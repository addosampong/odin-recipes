Yellow Squash and Corn Sauté

Description

Making perfect homemade lasagna doesn’t have to be tedious. It is as simple as combining the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings, and baking the mixture.

Ingredients

  • 2 ears corn, husked and cleaned
  • 2 yellow squash, diced
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons butter, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley (Optional)
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Gather all ingredients.
  3. Place corn into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, 10 to 15 minutes. Drain and set aside to cool.
  4. While the corn is cooling, place squash into a skillet and add water. Cook over medium heat until tender, 5 to 10 minutes. Drain any excess water from the skillet.
  5. Cut kernels off the cobs. Stir corn, butter, parsley, salt, and pepper into squash; cook, stirring occasionally, until heated through, about 5 minutes.
